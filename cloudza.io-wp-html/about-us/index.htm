About Us

CloudZA is a South African based Cloud Professional Services firm working on making organizations Cloud Native. Our mission is to assist organizations with their cloud journey.

Get in touch

What We Do

Our certified and skilled cloud engineers are able to provide assistance with your digital transformation, modern web application development and deployment. Allowing for agility and scalability in your organization, that will benefit your growth strategy.

Our team is made up of enthusiastic Cloud Architects, DevOps Engineers and System Administrators. With over a decade of combined experience we bring a unique skill set from on-premise data centres, training and development to finance and insurance services.

We strive for customer excellence in everything that we do. Our team is dedicated to understanding, simplifying powerful cloud technologies and increasing productivity by finding the most optimized way to run your organization’s workloads.

Our Vision

 

CloudZA’s vision is to be positioned as the go-to cloud professional services provider.

Providing migration services and managed IT support to businesses, providing efficient ways to run their workloads in the cloud and providing opportunities to skilled individuals.

Our Mission

 

CloudZA’s mission is to drive cloud awareness and to assist organizations with their cloud adoption journey, bridging the skills gap by working with organizations to understand their business and provide a migration plan to implement cloud based solutions.
